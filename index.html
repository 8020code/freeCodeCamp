Christopher Crosby "Chris" Farley was an American actor and comedian, who was known for his loud and energetic comedic style

Here's a time line of Chris Farley's life:

  • 1964 Farley was born in Madison, Wisconsin.
  • 1990 Along with Chris Rock, Farley was one of two new Saturday Night Live cast members.
  • 1992 Farley made his film debut as a security guard in Wayne's World.
  • 1995 Landed his first lead role in the hit Tommy Boy, in which he co-starred with friend and comic partner David Spade.
  • 1997 Headlined in Beverly Hills Ninja.
  • 1997 Chris Farley was found dead in his Chicago apartment after an extreme binge of substance use and party-hopping.
Quote

“I used to think that you could get to a level of success where the laws of the universe didn't apply. But they do. It's still life on life's terms, not on movie-star terms. I still have to work at relationships. I still have to work on my weight and some of my other demons.”

Chris Farley
